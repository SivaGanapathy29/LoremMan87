Faith, Hope and Charity - Our Motto


What makes us unique? Diversity, Fun & Giving

Our brethren come from diverse backgrounds – from photographers to businessmen, from doctors to IT professionals – everyone is welcome and is treated with equal warmth and respect.

FUN

Though we take our rituals quite seriously, we also have quite a bit of fun. From boisterous festive boards to outstation trips and just generally hanging out – we are known for our unique brand of camaraderie and friendship

GIVING

We believe in giving back to the society and have taken up various charitable initiatives. From blankets donation during winters to homeless to blood donation and food donation to migrant workers during COVID Lockdowns – our brethren have been at the forefront of charity in the Bangalore area
